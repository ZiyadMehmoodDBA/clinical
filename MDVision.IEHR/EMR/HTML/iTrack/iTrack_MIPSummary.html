What is MIPS
MIPS is a new approach to paying for Medicare Part B items and services in which clinicians may receive an increase or decrease in their payments based on performance.Performance is measured through the data clinicians report in four areas - Quality, Improvement Activities, Advancing Care Information, and Cost. We designed MIPS to update and consolidate previous programs, including: Medicare Meaningful Use (MU), Physician Quality Reporting System (PQRS), and the Value-Based Payment Modifier (VBM).

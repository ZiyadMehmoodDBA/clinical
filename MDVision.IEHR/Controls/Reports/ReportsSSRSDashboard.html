Welcome to the Data Mining Reports:



MDVision data mining reports provide essential decision-making knowledge of all your practice transactions and patient encounters. With rich search criteria options, users have the ability to search for and transfer desired information into paper or electronic format and create printable color reports that are exportable to MS Word, MS Excel, and Rich Text Format. These reports are organized into logical groups to facilitate advanced searching. The Reports Module uses self-explanatory names that reveal the purpose and functionality of each report. Enterprise reporting features allow practices to better manage their internal billing, accounting, clinical and practice management processes. The process for using MDVision data mining reports is simple and intuitive. Users simply click on the name of the appropriate report and then follow the same search process used elsewhere in the application to locate patients, appointments, procedures and other information. Online Help is available to explain the purpose, flow and the expected information for each Report. To access this help, simply click on the question mark (?) in the upper right-hand corner of each report screen. In the event that you have any difficulty viewing a report, please contact your Practice Administrator.
